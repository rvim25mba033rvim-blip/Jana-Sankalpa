Our Story

Founded in 2010, Jana Sankalpa Seva Charitable Trust was born from the simple yet powerful conviction that every individual — regardless of caste, creed, or economic background — deserves dignity, opportunity, and a life free from deprivation.

Starting with a small group of passionate volunteers in rural Karnataka, we have grown into a trusted organization working across education, health, women empowerment, environment, and livelihood development.

Integrity

We act with transparency and accountability in all we do.

Inclusion

We embrace diversity and serve all without discrimination.

What We Do

Our Programs

Holistic initiatives that address root causes of inequality and vulnerability.

Education for All

Providing quality education to underprivileged children through scholarships, learning centres, skill development, and digital literacy programs.

  • Free tuition centres in 40+ villages
  • Scholarships for girls' higher education
  • Digital literacy for rural youth
Learn More →

Community Healthcare

Ensuring accessible primary healthcare through mobile medical camps, health awareness drives, and mother-child wellness programs.

  • Monthly free health camps
  • Maternal & child nutrition support
  • Mental health awareness drives
Learn More →

Women Empowerment

Supporting women through self-help groups, vocational training, legal aid, and leadership development to achieve economic independence.

  • Self-help groups across 80 villages
  • Vocational & entrepreneurship training
  • Legal aid & counselling
Learn More →

Environment & Sustainability

Promoting environmental stewardship through tree plantation drives, waste management, water conservation and green community initiatives.

  • 50,000+ trees planted
  • Rainwater harvesting projects
  • Clean village campaigns
Learn More →

Livelihood Development

Creating sustainable income opportunities for marginalised families through skill training, microfinance linkages and market access support.

  • Skill training for 2,000+ youth
  • Microfinance linkage programs
  • Farmer support & crop planning
Learn More →

Disaster Relief

Providing immediate relief and long-term rehabilitation support to communities affected by floods, droughts, and other natural calamities.

  • Rapid emergency response team
  • Relief kit distribution
  • Shelter & rehabilitation support
