Who We Are

Compassion in Action

Jana Sankalpa Seva Charitable Trust (R) — ನಿರಾಶ್ರಿತರ ಆಶ್ರಮ — is a registered charitable organisation dedicated to uplifting the destitute, elderly, and differently-abled in and around Nelamangala Taluk, Bengaluru Rural District.

Founded by Smt. Jyothi C., the Trust has become a beacon of hope — offering shelter, nutrition, healthcare, and dignity to those who need it most. Recognised by the Karnataka Government for exemplary service to the disabled community on World Disability Day 2016.

Our Journey

What We Have Achieved

From humble beginnings to a government-recognised institution — every milestone reflects the power of collective compassion.

2016

🏅 Karnataka Government Recognition

On 3 December 2016 — World Disability Day — the Karnataka Government (Bengaluru Rural District) honoured Smt. Jyothi S. for rendering exemplary service in the field of disability welfare.

📍 Bhagat Singh Ground, Doddaballapur
2021

🌟 Praja Seva Kannadada Ratna Award

On 15 December 2021, Smt. C. Jyothiravaru was conferred the prestigious "Praja Seva Kannadada Ratna" award at Nelamangala Kannada Habba – Karnataka Rajyotsava, organised by Karnataka Janaseva (R).

✨ Karnataka Rajyotsava Celebration, Nelamangala
2023

💐 Nenapin Kanika — Basavanna Jayanti

On 19 February 2023, the Trust hosted a special memorial offering at Sri Bisavanna Devara Matha, Nelamangala — a tribute to the ideals of equality and social harmony inspired by Basavanna.

📍 Sri Bisavanna Devara Matha, Nelamangala
2024–25

🏠 Niraashritara Ashrama Expansion

Registered as NMG-4-00016-2024-25, the Trust expanded the Ashrama — providing shelter, food, clothing, and care for the elderly and destitute. Regular community meals now reach hundreds of beneficiaries monthly.

📍 Pragati Layout, Ganeshanagudi, Nelamangala – 562123
Our Purpose

Vision, Mission & What We Do

Every programme we run is rooted in our belief that every human being deserves dignity, shelter, and care — regardless of their circumstances.

🌅

Our Vision

A society where no person is left abandoned — where the destitute, elderly, and differently-abled are sheltered, cared for, and treated with the dignity that every human life deserves. We envision a Bengaluru Rural District free from neglect, powered by collective compassion.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide free shelter, nutritious food, healthcare support, and emotional well-being to the destitute, aged, and differently-abled through sustainable community partnerships, government collaboration, and volunteer-driven programmes — upholding Kannada culture and the value of seva.
