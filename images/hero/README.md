## Image/Video Guidelines

### For Images
- **Format**: JPG for photos
- **Size**: Under 1MB
- **Dimensions**: Full HD (1920x1080px) or higher for hero banners
- **Content**: Inspiring photos of the ashrama, activities, or beneficiaries

### For Videos
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- **Size**: Optimize to be under 5MB for web
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Duration**: 20-60 seconds, suitable for looping
- **Content**: Should be uplifting and showcase the trust's impact
- **Audio**: Muted (video plays without sound)
